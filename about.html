Who we are

Here at plant academy we understand the difficulties of becoming new plant parents. My team and I created this website to help with everything you need to know about common indoor plants to provide the best care for your new plants!

Why own plants?

We think that having plants in your home is the key to a successful life.

Plants offer a variety of benefits including:

  • Purifying the air.
  • Help reduce stress
  • Provide healthy produce
  • Add asthetic value to your home
